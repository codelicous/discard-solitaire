Game Rules:

Objective:

Remove cards from the tableau until only the four kings remain.

Setup:

  • Shuffle a 52-card deck.
  • Deal four cards faceup in a row (tableau) and place the remaining cards facedown (stockpile).

Play:

  1. Discard cards higher in value than another card of the same suit.
  2. Deal four new cards from the stockpile when no more cards can be discarded.
  3. Move any tableau card to an empty space if one is created.
  4. Win when only the four kings remain.
